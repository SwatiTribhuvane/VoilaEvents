Top Unique Wedding Theme Ideas – 2022

The importance of Indian wedding theme ideas cannot be overstated. As a result, every woman in India fantasizes about being married in the most opulent manner conceivable. However, a large wedding entails a lot of responsibility. The most crucial element in planning a wedding is deciding on a theme. Therefore, no wedding is complete without a spectacular theme! If you’re getting married and can’t decide on a theme, these Indian wedding theme ideas will come in handy.

Check out some Trendiest Wedding Themes For 2022 and how to evaluate if they’re right for you. Before getting to know the most loved themes let’s first understand

Make sure these factors are there on your wedding theme for your wedding!

Get the guest talking:

It should be immediately identifiable by your visitors. The guest who is there at the event should get what the theme is. It should not leave them confused.

Reflects YOUR style:

It should be unique to you. When guests enter, they should think, “this is So you”. The theme should be written your style all over it.

Stand Out:

It should be noticeable. Even if it has the same floral theme going around for centuries, it should be salient in appearance. Make sure it is extraordinary.

Now, read ahead some of the unique wedding themes to make your wedding extraordinary.

The Rajasthani Theme

A Rajwada theme is a royal Rajasthani wedding that transports you back to the Rajputana era. This motif is vibrant and joyful, with colorful rangolis, pots, and props with a traditional taste like bandhani. The bride wears a decked palki, while the groom rides a ghodi. So, lots of fireworks and dal baati churma can help make it even more unforgettable.

A Disney Fairytale Theme

Why not commemorate your princess’s most memorable moments in the style of a classic fairy tale? Decorate with flowers, a little Cinderella, antique candelabras, gold urns, and happily ever after signs to incorporate the theme.

Use dazzling lights dripping from a tree in conjunction with a grand ceremony arch. Make it more intimate by using draped fabric. Tell your fairytale in a musical evening and close the event with a spectacular fireworks show. Most Popular Wedding Themes use a projector and big screens that showcase pictures and wedding stories during the event.

The Floral Theme

When it comes to wedding themes, a floral wedding theme will always be the front-runner as it’s very versatile and excellent for a summer wedding. Floral mandaps, in combination with floral props, set the mood for a delightful summer wedding. This theme can make any space look extravaganza. Even if you have a small wedding ceremony, this simple wedding theme idea cannot disappoint you.

The Bollywood Theme

If you can’t get enough Bollywood in your life, make it a wedding theme and let the drama begin. So, if you want to spice up your wedding reception, opt for a Bollywood theme. From music to clothing to décor, you can personalize everything to represent your favorite movies and stars in this stirring Indian Wedding Theme. As a result, use large mirrors embellished with dazzling lights to add glitz and drama to your celebrations. Also, a picture booth with life-size cutouts of popular figures would entertain the guests well.
