Ultimate Indian Wedding Planning Checklist

Indian weddings are a ceremonious affair that requires months to prepare in order to execute one perfect event of your life. Planning an Indian wedding without a precise checklist can be difficult, from selecting the venue to reserving caterers, decorators, and invitation cards, among other things. Unless you hire wedding planners that specialize in weddings.

We have created this blog to give a cheat sheet to the Ultimate Wedding Planning Checklist, that works! After massive research and collaborating tips on wedding planning from the legendary planner, we have below formed a checklist ready for you to take advantage of.

Scroll through to make the planning process easier and save significant time from the busy schedule you must be having.

Selecting tentative Dates:

We know you are a perfectionist, therefore to make things easier to manage you need to decide a lucky date (tentative) that you want to tie the knots on. In any progressive wedding planning checklist for an Indian bride, you will realize that a season can account for many things in your wedding. For instance, the dress, the venue, decorations, and the list goes on.

Get your Budget Straight!:

As soon as you set the date for the most anticipated day of your life – anxiety and excitement levels skyrocket. And, in most cases, the budget remains the most pressing worry. Some of the most primary considerations during the early stages of your wedding planning are how much to spend, where to spend it, and when to spend it. As a result, it’s critical to finish the budget before moving on to the next item on your wedding planning to-do list!

Where is your Wedding? – Select Venue:

Since having an extravagant wedding has become such a popular trend, there’s almost no chance of securing the ideal venue because most wedding venues are booked months in advance. As a result, begin looking for your dream wedding site at least 10 to 11 months ahead of time.

It is considered crucial in every wedding planning checklist for Indian brides to check for variables such as location, ambiance, space, and venue size, as well as the estimated number of guests that will attend the ceremony.

Who’s cooking at your weddings? – Pick Caters:

Connect with the finest catering service providers in the area and choose one for your big day! If the food tastes great, a wedding day is hailed as a tremendous success. You can play around with your food by serving a range of cuisines. If you want to be safe, you can always stick to the standard options.

Who’s clicking pictures? – Book a Photographer And Videographer:

While you are creating the best memories of your life, someone must be taking pictures to save them forever. You won’t want to forget how happy you looked on your big day. While candid photography has piqued the interest of the majority of the population, some of us still prefer the conventional method of collecting memories. This is a star-point for every Indian wedding planning checklist for the groom and bride.

Booking a photographer and videographer is essential, while there are some amazing photographers out there who can see your vision of a wedding and have the ability to capture it can be a task to find. Make sure you’ve decided whether you want theme, candid, or old-school photography, and book the photographer and videographer as soon as feasible. Make sure you see and compare the work of your potential selections.
