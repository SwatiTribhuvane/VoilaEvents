WHAT WE DO AT

sterling banquets hall-
Nerul, Navi Mumbai

Epitome of Opulence For the Event of Your Life!

Sterling Banquet & Lawn is located in Nerul East, Mumbai.It has one beatiful banquet hall of capacity upto 1500 people can manage in floating. All venues are centralised ac, attached dress changing rooms and separate dining area.Sterling Banquet & Lawn has spacious car spacious parking which can park upto 50 cars. Outside food / catering is not allowed, inhouse catering facility is available and guest need to pay per plate system. Starting menu price per plate or buffet price in Sterling Banquet & Lawn is Rs.525. People who are looking for both veg and not veg food, this venue is suitable. Liquor is not allowed at this venue .

Nearest localities to Sterling Banquet & Lawn are Seawoods, Navi Mumbai, Nerul, Nerul East, Nerul - Navi Mumbai, Belapur, CBD Belapur, Sanpada, Turbhe, Kharghar, which can reach by bus. Sterling Banquet & Lawn is a perfect place for any family or corporate event.Events permitted at Sterling Banquet & Lawn are Engagement, Wedding, Reception, Sangeet, Mehandi, Baby Shower, Wedding Anniversary, Bachelor Party, Baptism, Birthday Party, Get-Together, Kitty Party, Naming Ceremony, Ring Ceremony, Freshers Party, Upanayanam .
